Welcome To Phoenix Synergize

We are a competitive, organized, and team-oriented Clash of Clans clan.

Our mission is to foster a supportive environment where every member can thrive, contribute, and achieve greatness together.

    1. Treat all members with respect. No bullying, harassment, or abusive language.

    2. More than 3 days of inactivity will be result in a kick without any warning.

    3. Donate requested troops promptly. Maintain a healthy donation ratio.

    4. Participate in clan games. Complete raid attack every week, Contribute gold to Prioritize tagged buildings.

    5. Members should not hop between clans. Loyalty is valued and expected.

    6. If you leave the clan without inform, will not be allowed to rejoin.

    7. If you plan to be inactive for an extended period Inform Co-leaders.

    8. If you get kicked out for inactive, you can rejoin the clan once you're active again.

    9. Repeatedly or frequently violating the rules will result in a ban from clan.
